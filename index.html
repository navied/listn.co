Listen to your friends' music, for free

The largest collection of music in the world!

Whether you use Spotify, Youtube, Rdio or just an iTunes account, Listn lets you combine your music libraries,
allowing you to explore and listen to your friends’ music, regardless of what music streaming service you are on.

Share your music

All of the music on your mobile device forms your Listn music library. Simply sign up and your music instantly becomes available to your friends and followers. Create playlists and heart songs from other people’s library to add them to your own.

Connect with friends

Listn gives you the ability to interact with music like you have never done before. Follow your friends’ music profiles and explore what they listen to, all in a simple stream.
